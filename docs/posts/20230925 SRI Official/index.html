In Loving Memory of Spencer Irvine

13 February 1988 - 24 September 2023

It is with profound sadness that we announce the untimely passing of a beloved young husband, Spencer Irvine, who left this world far too soon. He was born on 13 February 1988, a day that would come to be cherished by those who had the privilege of knowing him. Spencer Irvine departed from our lives on 24 September 2023, leaving behind a void that can never be filled.

Spencer Irvine was more than just a husband; he was a source of unwavering love, strength, and joy to his family, friends, and all who crossed his path. His life was a testament to kindness, compassion, and the ability to bring smiles to the faces of everyone he encountered.

As a husband, Spencer was a true partner in every sense of the word. His love for his spouse was boundless, and he dedicated himself wholeheartedly to the happiness and well-being of their shared life. Their love story was one of deep connection and a bond that will transcend the boundaries of time.

Spencer had an infectious zest for life. He had a passion for is an avid history buff and enjoys touring historic sites and was always eager to share his enthusiasm with those around him. Whether he was pursuing his own interests or supporting his loved   ones in their endeavors, his presence was a beacon of encouragement and inspiration.

In addition to his warm heart and joyful spirit, Spencer possessed an unwavering sense of responsibility and dedication. He was a loving son, a caring brother, and a loyal friend. His presence in our lives brought comfort, laughter, and a sense of unity that will be profoundly missed.

While Spencer may have left this world far too soon, his memory will live on in the hearts of all who knew him. His love, his laughter, and the beautiful moments shared together will forever be etched in our minds.

As we mourn the loss of this extraordinary young husband, let us also celebrate the incredible life he lived and the love he shared.  Spencer will remain forever in our thoughts and prayers.

A memorial service to honor and remember Spencer Irvine will be held on 30 September 2023 at 10AM MST at Mesa, AZ. In lieu of flowers, the family kindly requests donations be made to Accuracy In Academia in Spencer Irvine’s memory.

Please join us in saying goodbye to a remarkable husband, son, brother, uncle and friend. Spencer Irvine’s legacy of love and kindness will continue to inspire us all.

With love and heartfelt condolences,

Uncle David, on behalf of Irvine and Nunez Family
